Academic Programs Offered

1. College Degree Programs (CHED)

  • Computer & Digital Technology: BS in Computer Science (BSCS), BS in Information Systems (BSIS), BS in Computer Engineering (BSCoE), Electronics Engineering.
  • Business & Accountancy: BS in Management Accounting (BSMA), BS in Internal Auditing (BSIA), Business Administration.
  • Health Sciences: BS in Medical Technology (BSMT), Nursing, and Healthcare Support courses.
  • Criminology: BS in Criminology, Industrial Security Management.
  • Education: Bachelor of Early Childhood Education (BECEd), Bachelor of Technical-Vocational Teacher Education (BTVTEd).
  • Hospitality & Tourism: BS in Hospitality Management (BSHM), BS in Tourism Management (BSTM).
  • Arts & Sciences: BA in Communication, BS in Psychology.

2. Technical-Vocational Programs (TESDA)

Fast-track certificate courses centered on immediate, job-ready practical skills spanning IT, health care, culinary fields, and engineering trades.

3. Senior High School (DepEd)

Grades 11 and 12 academic and technical-vocational tracks. ICCT honors the DepEd Senior High School Voucher program (frequently promoting "No Top-Ups" policies depending on terms).

Student FAQ

How can I enroll in courses?
Start by visiting the admissions office or applying online through the dedicated student portal. After your application is reviewed, a guidance counselor will help you select the best program and schedule.
What are the payment options?
ICCT accepts on-campus payments, bank deposit slips, and online payment gateways for tuition and fees. Contact the accounting office for installment plans and receipt verification.
Can I join even if I am a working student?
Yes. ICCT offers flexible scheduling, evening classes, and blended learning delivery designed specifically for employed learners.
How do I access online learning resources?
Use the digital campus portal to log in and access course materials, virtual classrooms, and e-service support. Your credentials are provided during enrollment.
